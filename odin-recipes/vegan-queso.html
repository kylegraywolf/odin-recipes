Vegan Queso

Description

This "queso" recipe leans more towards the type you would find in a tex-mex restaurant rather than what you would find in an order of nachos at a baseball game. The recipe has a mild flavor that can be easily adjusted with different spices or the addition of jalapenos, diced tomatoes, etc.

Yields roughly 2 cups, or one tomato sauce jar of queso, and takes about 10 minutes to complete.

Ingredients - Wet

  • 2 Cups plant milk
  • 4 TBSP neutral oil
  • 1 TBSP shiro miso
  • 1 dried chipotle pepper, whole
  • 1 TBSP adobo sauce

Ingredients - Dry

  • 1-3 TBSP corn starch
  • 4-1/2 TBSP nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground mustard
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Steps

  1. Combine all dry ingredients into a bowl and mix.
  2. Add all wet ingredients to a large saucepan. Bring to boil over high heat while stirring, the mixture will foam quickly.
  3. Transfer to a blender and blend until smooth. Add in dry ingredients, and blend until smooth. Queso will thicken as it cools.
